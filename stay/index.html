Your Stay at The Reynolds Mansion

Eleven thoughtfully appointed rooms across two buildings, each with its own character, private bath, and a four-course gourmet breakfast prepared by our classically trained chef-innkeeper every morning.

The Main House · Est. 1847

Reynolds Residence

Eight guest rooms across three floors of the original 1847 mansion. Every room features antique furnishings, private baths, and the kind of architectural detail — twelve-foot ceilings, gas fireplaces, original brick — that only comes with nearly 180 years of history.

Queen and King rooms · Floors 1–3 · Gas fireplaces · Claw foot tubs · Keurig & Illy Espresso · Writing desks · Blue Ridge Mountain views

Private Suites · Dog Friendly

The Carriage House

Three spacious suites in a separate building just across the courtyard. Each suite offers more room and privacy, with gas fireplaces, Illy Espresso, mini refrigerators, and a large covered porch overlooking the estate gardens and mountains.

King suites · Jacuzzi tubs · Sofa seating · Private deck · Mini fridge · Dogs welcome ($40/day + tax, Carriage House only)

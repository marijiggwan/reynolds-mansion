Chef-Prepared Gourmet Breakfast

Included with every stay  ·  Served daily at 9:00 AM

This Is Not Eggs on Toast

Every morning, our chef-innkeeper prepares a four-course gourmet breakfast from scratch. Each course is plated individually and served on heirloom china in the grand dining room of an 1847 mansion — under a crystal chandelier, beside a fireplace that has been warming this room for nearly 180 years.

This is not a buffet. It is not continental. It is a professionally prepared meal by a classically trained chef who spent decades in some of the world's leading hospitality kitchens before choosing to cook for twenty-eight guests instead of eight hundred.

Your Chef-Innkeeper

Prepared by Paul Manshon

With decades of experience in world-class hospitality kitchens, Paul brings a professional chef's precision to an intimate setting. Every dish is prepared fresh each morning — from hand-selected seasonal produce to house-made sauces and pastries.

The menus change daily. Repeat guests never have the same breakfast twice. And the presentation on vintage china, crystal stemware, and silver service reflects the same care that goes into every course.

The Four-Course Experience

Every Morning, Four Courses

Menus rotate daily and change with the seasons. Here is a glimpse of what a morning at The Reynolds Mansion looks like.

First Course

The Starter

A house-made amuse to begin — seasonal fruit smoothies, chilled soups, or a palate-opening course that signals this morning will be different from any breakfast you have had before.

Second Course

Seasonal Fruit

Fresh seasonal fruit, artfully arranged and served with house-made granola and yogurt. Not a fruit cup from a container — hand-selected, hand-cut, and plated with the same care as every other course.

Third Course

The Intermezzo

A palate cleanser between fruit and main — poached pears with cream and coulis, house-baked scones with berry sauce, or a delicate pastry that belongs in a patisserie window. This course is where guests first realize they are in a chef's hands.

Fourth Course

The Main

The centerpiece of the morning. Eggs Benedict on house-made potato cakes with hollandaise. Blueberry pancake stacks with warm compote. Savory crepes, French toast, or whatever the chef has planned — different every day, always from scratch.

The Setting

The Grand Dining Room

Breakfast is served in the mansion's grand dining room, where a mahogany table seats up to fourteen guests beneath a crystal chandelier. When we have a full house, additional seating is set in the intimate parlor — equally warm, equally beautiful.

On warmer mornings, we may offer seating on the porch overlooking the Blue Ridge Mountains. But wherever you sit, the experience is the same: four courses, fine china, and a morning you will remember long after you have checked out.

Served daily at 9:00 AM  ·  Included with every stay  ·  Please let us know if you plan to skip breakfast on any morning during your stay

Common Questions

What is breakfast like at The Reynolds Mansion?

Breakfast at The Reynolds Mansion is a four-course gourmet meal prepared from scratch each morning by our classically trained chef-innkeeper. Courses include a house-made starter, seasonal fruit, an intermezzo (pastry or palate cleanser), and a plated main course. Everything is served on fine china in the grand dining room. It is included with every stay and served daily at 9:00 AM.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

We do our best to accommodate dietary needs with advance notice. Please contact us before your stay to discuss specific dietary restrictions so our chef can plan accordingly. If we are unable to fully accommodate your needs, we will refer you to a fellow bed and breakfast in our association that specializes in dietary accommodations.

Is breakfast the same every day?

No. Menus change daily and rotate with the seasons. Repeat guests never have the same breakfast twice. Our chef draws on decades of professional kitchen experience to create menus that feature seasonal, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Where is breakfast served?

Breakfast is served in the mansion's grand dining room, which seats up to fourteen guests at a mahogany table beneath a crystal chandelier. When we have a full house, additional seating is arranged in the intimate parlor. On warmer mornings, porch seating with Blue Ridge Mountain views may also be available.

Dogs Welcome

Traveling With Your Dog?

The Carriage House is the only pet-friendly accommodation on the estate. We welcome dogs of all sizes — a non-refundable fee of $40 per day plus taxes applies.

We prefer that your dog accompanies you when away from the inn. If left in the suite for short periods, please use a portable crate. Dogs must be on leash and under your control at all times on the grounds. Please clean up after your pet and avoid using inn towels for dirty paws — we will gladly provide something suitable.

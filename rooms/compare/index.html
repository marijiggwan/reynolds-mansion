Compare Our Rooms

Every room includes a private bath, complimentary Wi-Fi, and a four-course gourmet breakfast each morning. Dogs are welcome in the Carriage House suites only.

The eight Reynolds Residence rooms are spread across three floors of the original 1847 mansion. First-floor rooms have no stairs from the entry. Second-floor rooms open onto a covered porch with Blue Ridge Mountain views. Third-floor rooms sit beneath the mansion's mansard rooflines and offer the most intimate, cozy footprint.

The three Carriage House suites — All That Jazz, Elephant Walk, and Blue Hydrangea — sit in a separate building twenty feet across the courtyard. Each is larger than the Residence rooms, with a gas fireplace, Jacuzzi tub, and a private deck or porch. The Carriage House is the only dog-friendly accommodation on the estate (35-pound limit, $40 per day plus tax).

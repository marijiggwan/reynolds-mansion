Getting Here

The Reynolds Mansion sits on Reynolds Mountain in north Asheville — a secluded mountain setting just ten minutes from downtown restaurants, galleries, and breweries.

Our Address

The Reynolds Mansion Bed & Breakfast Inn
100 Reynolds Heights
Asheville, NC 28804

Phone: (828) 258-1111

From Major Highways

From I-240: Take Exit 3B for UNC Asheville / Merrimon Avenue (US-25 North). Continue north on Merrimon Avenue approximately 2 miles. Turn left onto Reynolds Mountain Road, then follow signs to Reynolds Heights.

From I-26: Take Exit 21 for US-25 North / Merrimon Avenue toward Weaverville. Continue north on Merrimon Avenue. Turn left onto Reynolds Mountain Road.

From the Blue Ridge Parkway: Exit at US-25 (Merrimon Avenue). Head north approximately 1.5 miles. Turn left onto Reynolds Mountain Road.

If your GPS has trouble finding us, call us at (828) 258-1111 and we'll guide you in.

Distances from the Mansion

The Reynolds Mansion is nestled in the mountains, but everything you need is close at hand. Downtown Asheville is ten minutes south. Biltmore Estate is about fifteen minutes. The Blue Ridge Parkway is practically at the doorstep.

Downtown Asheville 10 minutes
Biltmore Estate 15 minutes
Blue Ridge Parkway 5 minutes
AVL Airport 30 minutes

Parking

Free private off-street parking is available for all guests, right on the property. No need to search for a spot or pay for a garage.

Flying In

Asheville Regional Airport (AVL) is approximately 30 minutes south of the Mansion and served by five airlines: Allegiant, American, Delta, Sun Country, and United. With nonstop service to 26 destinations, getting to the mountains is easier than most people expect.

Reynolds Village

Welcome to the neighborhood. Reynolds Village is the only walkable shopping and dining center in north Asheville — and it's just a five-minute stroll from your door at The Reynolds Mansion. All the convenience of downtown, with free parking, tree-lined streets, and none of the noise.

Dining & Drinks

From Smash Burgers to Sommelier-Level Wine

Sports Bar & Restaurant

The Village Porch

North Asheville's neighborhood hangout. Smash burgers, 23 craft taps, live music, trivia nights, and enough TVs to catch every game. Family-friendly inside, dog-friendly on the patio.

thevillageporch.com →

Wine Bistro & Restaurant

Quench! Wine Bistro

A collaboration of Metro Wines and Chef Sam Etheridge. Over 20 wines by the glass, seasonal menus, cheese and charcuterie, and tasting menus with pairings. Named Best Restaurant in Woodfin for 2025.

quenchavl.com →
